Jakub Hampl

Hi! I'm a first-year PhD student at the University of Aberdeen, working under the supervision of Richard Hepworth-Young. I'm part of the 'Topology of Algebras' research project.

I'm interested in algebraic topology, homotopy theory, operads, and homological stability. I spend most of my time thinking about topology of diagram algebras.
